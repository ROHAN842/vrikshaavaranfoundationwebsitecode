Who We are

Vrikshaavaran is a Non profit organization who is continously working in the field of saving mother earth by planting more and more number of trees also saving trees from being cut inorder to do proper conservation of trees.

What we do

Vrikshaavaran is a campaign that uses technology for executing conservation in the best way possible with the help of geo-mapping etc. ultimately leading to the maintenance of the eco-balance. Also organizing various tree plantation events on hilly areas and vacant places of the district.

How we work ?

Vrikshaavaran will provide the placeholders and technology like geo-mapping to the experts for their research of organized planting. In this way, we can come up with a plan that will eventually lead to the good old times, when there were so many fruitful trees, that the hungry could easily feed themselves by picking up a few fruits out the many, right from the ground. Vrikshaavaran dreams of leading to a beautiful green habitat, and leaving the same for our generations to come.
